Questions Answered
Everything You Need
to Know Before We Talk.
How much do your services cost? +
Our packages are custom-scoped to each client — because a one-size-fits-all retainer rarely fits anyone. After your discovery call, we'll present a proposal tailored to your goals, budget, and timeline. Most retainers start from $850/month and go up to $2,000/month depending on the scope, services, and level of support your brand needs.
How quickly will I see results? +
Paid ads typically show meaningful data within 2–4 weeks. Organic content, SEO, and email marketing tend to compound over 60–90 days. We're transparent about timelines from day one, so you always know what to expect and when.
What industries do you work with? +
We specialize in service businesses, personal brands, coaches, consultants, ecommerce brands, and startups. We've worked across wellness, beauty, fashion, business services, real estate, food & beverage, and more. If you're an ambitious brand ready to grow, we're likely a fit.
How do you communicate with clients? +
Every client gets a dedicated Slack channel, a shared project dashboard, and scheduled weekly or bi-weekly check-in calls. You'll never wonder what we're working on — everything is documented and transparent.
Do I need to provide ad spend budget separately? +
Yes. Our management fee is separate from your ad spend. Ad budgets go directly to the ad platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok). We'll recommend a minimum ad budget based on your goals — and help you scale it as campaigns prove ROI.
What are your contract terms? +
We typically work on 3-month minimum engagements, as this allows enough time to see meaningful results and properly optimize campaigns. After that, we move to month-to-month with 30 days notice. No surprise lock-ins.
Who approves content before it goes live? +
You always have final approval. We use a structured content review workflow — you'll receive content calendars, creative assets, and captions for review before anything is published or launched.
Can you guarantee specific results? +
We don't make guarantees — and any agency that does should be questioned. What we do guarantee is: strategy built on real data, transparent reporting, proactive communication, and relentless optimization until your campaigns perform.
